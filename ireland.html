VoC Performance: Efficiency & Equality (including Ireland)

Ireland’s headline GDP is distorted by multinational profit-shifting, especially after 2015. This chart includes Ireland using a spliced income series: WID national income per adult for 1980–2014, then CSO modified GNI (GNI*) from 2015 onward converted from EUR to USD per capita (ECB annual exchange rates).
