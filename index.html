Political Economy Typology

Varieties of Capitalism

Varieties of Capitalism (VoC) classifies advanced market economies into two ideal types—liberal market economies (LMEs) & coordinated market economies (CMEs)—that differ systematically in how firms interact with the institutions of capital & labor.
